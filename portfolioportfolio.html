'WHEN IT RAINS, IT POURS' PROJECTION DESIGNS

Under the School of Theatre and Dance at Illinois State University 'When it Rains, it Pours' was staged on pre-professional dancers. These projection designs were created for professional modern dancer and choreographer Morgan 'Mo' Williams; founder and director of Water Street Dance Milwaukee.

STANDARDS GRAPHICS MANUAL FOR SOCIAL GOOD

Kinetic Dance Apparel is a mock Clothing Company that focuses on providing clothing options that make dancers feel confident and comfortable in looser fitted athletic clothing.

BREAKING MAGAZINE SPREAD REDESIGN

Diggit Magazine’s article 'Breaking Global Cultural Boundaries Though Dance' redesigned page spreads bring light to the history of 'breaking' in a creative and fun manner.

'KOTA THE FRIEND' HTML AND CSS WEBSITE

Inspired by lyric rapper Kota the Friend, this coded website mimics his iPhone featuring his discography, albums, and streaming services. Front-end and back-end was developed using HTML and CSS.

STARBUCKS EPHEMERAL VARIETY

For a school project, I rebranded Starbucks because I admire their values and social impact as a company. Having worked at Starbucks, I gained an inside perspective that influenced my creative decisions.

EXPECTATIONS VS. REALITY PERFORMANCE ART

‘Expectations vs Reality’ is a multimedia piece that challenges one's predisposed notions of reality. It is a DADA inspired work with themes of playfulness, layered ideas, and avant-garde concepts. Time, color, and sound are all explored in this combination of projections with modern dance.

'FRAGMENTED' PROJECTION DESIGNS

Under founder and artistic director Morgan 'Mo' Williams, for Water Street Dance Milwaukee, I did the projection designs for his company modern dancers. 'Fragmented' is an evening-length work that incorporates seven chapters. Chapter I. Entity, II. Speaking to My Younger Self, III. Broken Veil, IV. “My God Aint This Yours” Ft. Brooklyn Llyod, V. Falling Into…, VI. Self ← Unseen, and VII. When it Rains, It Pours (restaged).

LANDSCAPE OF TIME IMMERSIVE PROJECTIONS

Landscape of Time is an immersive projection installation that plays with concepts of time, transportation, and postmodern imagery. Covid-19 changed my perception of time as this piece drives that idea of time as a landscape rather than an arrow.

'THIS IS A CENSORED EXHIBITION' GALLERY

On February 3 of 2023 a train carrying tons of hazardous and toxic chemicals derailed in Palestine, Ohio. This exhibition is a product of the little amount that we know.

GRAPHIC COMPILATION MIXED MEDIA

This is a compilation of media from graphic design posters, logos, animated logos, rotoscoping and dance reels.
