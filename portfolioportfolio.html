EXPECTATIONS VS. REALITY

Expectations vs. Reality is a video filmed on the Filmic Pro app with Premiere Pro used in post-production. This video is inspired from the Surrealist Movement giving the video a dream-like quality. Time, color, and sound are all explored challenging one’s predisposed notions of reality.

'KOTA THE FRIEND' HTML AND CSS WEBSITE

This website created using HTML and CSS was inspired by lyric rapper Kota the Friend. It mimics his iPhone with his discography, albums, and streaming services featured.

'BANANA CLIP' KINETIC TYPE MUSIC VIDEO

This kinetic type music video visually decorates the lyrics to artist Miguel’s song ‘Banana Clip’. A combination of illustrations and text is used. After Effects, Photoshop, and Illustrator were all used in the creation of this work.

BREAKING MAGAZINE SPREADS

These magazine spreads redesign Diggit Magazine’s article on Breaking and Hip-Hop. Adobe InDesign and Illustrator were used in the process.
