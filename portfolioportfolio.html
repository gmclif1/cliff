EXPECTATIONS VS. REALITY

‘Expectations vs Reality’ is a multimedia piece that challenges ones predisposed notions of reality. It is a DADA inspired work with themes of playfulness, layered ideas, and avant-garde concepts. Time, color, and sound are all explored in this combination of projections with modern dance.

PROJECTION DESIGNS FOR MODERN DANCE

These projection designs were created for Modern dance choreographer Morgan "Mo" Williams director of Water Street Dance Milwaukee. Under the School of Theatre and Dance at Illinois State 'When it Rains, it Pours' was staged on pre-professional dancers.

'KOTA THE FRIEND' HTML AND CSS WEBSITE

This website created using HTML and CSS was inspired by lyric rapper Kota the Friend. It mimics his iPhone with his discography, albums, and streaming services featured.

STANDARDS GRAPHICS MANUAL FOR SOCIAL GOOD

Kinetic Dance Apparel is a mock Clothing Company that focuses on giving clothing options that make the dancer feel as confident and comfortable as possible through looser fitted athletic clothing.

'BANANA CLIP' KINETIC TYPE MUSIC VIDEO

This kinetic type music video visually decorates the lyrics to artist Miguel’s song ‘Banana Clip’. A combination of illustrations and text is used. After Effects, Photoshop, and Illustrator were all used in the creation of this work.

BREAKING MAGAZINE SPREADS

These magazine spreads redesign Diggit Magazine’s article on Breaking and Hip-Hop. Adobe InDesign and Illustrator were used in the process.

ROTOSCOPING VIRTUAL DANCE CONCERT 'PIVOT'

With the School of Creative Technology and Theatre and Dance in my Motion Graphics class to create digital designs with rotoscoping for the Virtual Spring Dance Concert titled 'Pivot'. I specifically worked on the piece titled 'Look for the Light!' choreographed by Greg Merriman. I also danced in this piece which gave me a special insight into both creative aspects.
