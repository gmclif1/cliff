ABOUT ME

I am a Junior at Illinois State University in the process of receiving a BA in Graphic Design and Creative Technology with a minor in Dance. I am interested in combining kinesthetic art and technology.

SKILLS

I am well versed in all the Adobe products with the most experience in Illustrator and Premiere Pro. I am also familiar with HTML and CSS. Some classes I have taken within my majors include Video, Digital Photography, Digital Media, Selected Topics in Arts Technology, and Graphic Design. I also am familiar with lighting for stage as I racked and focused lights for the Illinois State Show ‘Ga-AD’.

In my free time I engage in activities including choreographing, scrapbooking, embroidering, listening to music, and watching the live arts. I also enjoy traveling and playing tennis.
