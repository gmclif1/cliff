When it Rains, it Pours

So blessed I had the opportunity to create projection designs for “When it rains, it pours” choreographed by Morgan 'Mo' Williams.

I created projection designs for modern dance choreographer Morgan Williams director of Water Street Dance Milwaukee. Under the School of Theatre and Dance at Illinois State he staged his work on pre-professional dancers. I worked closely with him and the dancers as creative assistant to make projection work that would further emphasize his choreography.
