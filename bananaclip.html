Banana Clip Music Video

This kinetic type music video is inspired from the song ‘Banana Clip’ by Miguel. The manipulation of type is the primary focus with some illustrations. After Effects, Photoshop, and Illustrator was used in the creation of this video. This was created during my Digital Media class Sophomore Year of College. I began with sketches and storyboarding. The transitions proved to be the most challenging part of this video being effective while keeping the viewer engaged.
