Breaking Magazine Spreads

This magazine cover and spreads redesigns Diggit Magazine’s Article on Breaking and how the Hip-Hop industry has changed from the 1970’s. Adobe InDesign was used creating this piece as well as Illustrator. All the text is from the original article but the visual imagery and stylization is not.
