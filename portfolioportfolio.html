PROJECTION DESIGNS FOR MODERN DANCE

Under the School of Theatre and Dance at Illinois State University 'When it Rains, it Pours' was staged on pre-professional dancers. These projection designs were created for professional modern dancer and choreographer Morgan "Mo" Williams director of Water Street Dance Milwaukee.

STANDARDS GRAPHICS MANUAL FOR SOCIAL GOOD

Kinetic Dance Apparel is a mock Clothing Company that focuses on providing clothing options that make dancers feel confident and comfortable in looser fitted athletic clothing.

LANDSCAPE OF TIME IMMERSIVE PROJECTIONS

Landscape of Time is an immersive projection installation that plays with concepts of time, transportation, and postmodern imagery.

EXPECTATIONS VS. REALITY

‘Expectations vs Reality’ is a multimedia piece that challenges one's predisposed notions of reality. It is a DADA inspired work with themes of playfulness, layered ideas, and avant-garde concepts. Time, color, and sound are all explored in this combination of projections with modern dance.

STARBUCKS REBRANDING

For a school project I rebranded Starbucks because I admire thier values and social impact as a company. Having worked at Starbucks, I gained an inside perspective that influenced my creative decisions.

BREAKING MAGAZINE SPREAD REDESIGN

Using Diggit Magazine’s article 'Breaking Global Cultural Boundaries Though Dance' I redesigned their page spreads using Adobe InDesign and Illustrator.

'KOTA THE FRIEND' HTML AND CSS WEBSITE

This website coded using HTML and CSS was inspired by lyric rapper Kota the Friend. It mimics his iPhone featuring his discography, albums, and streaming services.
